# Agentic IDEs Summary

This document summarizes the changes, specifications, and prompts generated during interactions with Agentic IDEs (specifically from the `doc/kiro_01` and `doc/antigravity_01` directories).

## Key Themes & Features

1. **Calendar and Timeline Views**
   - **Prompts**: See [Calendar Prompts](../user-prompts/Calendar_Prompts.md) and [Timeline Prompts](../user-prompts/Timeline_Prompts.md).
   - **Details**: Extensive work was done on the Task Calendar View and Activity Timeline View in `kiro_01`. Refer to [Pending Tasks](../sections/Pending%20Tasks) and [Timeline sections](../sections/timeline) for design and requirement documents.

2. **Notices & Push Notifications**
   - **Prompts**: See [Notice Prompts](../user-prompts/Notice_Prompts.md).
   - **Details**: Work included CR Notice workflows and push notifications. See the detailed implementations in [Notice sections](../sections/notice).

3. **User Management & Role Privileges**
   - **Details**: Work on faculty role privileges and general user management UI updates. Located in [Profile and User Management](../sections/profile%20and%20user%20management).

4. **Event Management**
   - **Prompts**: See [Events Prompts](../user-prompts/Events_Prompts.md).
   - **Details**: Specific UI tweaks and integration for role events. Refer to [Events sections](../sections/events).

## Best Practices & Key Takeaways
- **Prompt Organization**: Group prompts logically by feature to maintain context for future iterations.
- **Specification Tracking**: Tie every LLM prompt to its corresponding requirements and design documentation to ensure end-to-end traceability.
